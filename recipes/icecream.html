Ice Cream

This cold delicacy fills us with joy

We love ice cream. When I finish getting these pages together we'll probably have some. Yep, that's how much we love ice cream.

List of Ingredients

  • Money
  • Transportation to grocery store
  • Wherewithall to pick best option
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Instructions

  1. Go to grocery store
  2. Pick a type of ice cream (TIP: Use all your wherewithall)
  3. Buy ice cream (TIP: Don't use all your money, save some for later...)
  4. Go home
  5. Scoop ice cream into bowl
  6. Get a spoon
  7. Enjoy!
