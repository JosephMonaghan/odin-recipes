Oatmeal

This is oatmeal, a delicious meal

This is oatmeal, which decidedly does not contain cheese (often). Despite this, it is an acceptable morning meal on occassion.

Ingredients Required

  • Chocolate
  • Cinammon
  • Oats
  • Extra chocolate
  • Bananas (sometimes)

Steps to prepare

  1. Add all chocolate to bowl
  2. Snack on it for a bit
  3. Heat a bit up with remaining ingredients
  4. Enjoy!
